Tok Tok Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tok Tok processes information in connection with its habit tracking, todo, memo, speech, focus timer, and notification features.

1. Information We Collect

Category Main items Source
Account data Email address, password-based sign-in data, and Google account profile information needed for authentication Sign-up, sign-in, Google Sign-In
User content Habits, todo lists and items, memos, speech documents, completion history, reminder settings, ordering, pinned state, and schedule data Entered or edited by the user
Settings data Notification preferences, home screen preference, hide-completed preference, UI density, theme, and related settings App settings and local storage
Device and session data Session ID, device ID, platform information, active session information, and FCM push token Login, session management, notification setup
Analytics and diagnostics Screen views, feature usage events, login method, crash logs, and error diagnostics Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users, maintain sessions, and detect abnormal access
  • To store and sync habits, todos, memos, speeches, and settings across devices
  • To send reminders, due-date notifications, and service notices
  • To analyze usage, improve features, and diagnose crashes or service issues
  • To respond to inquiries and provide operational notices

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase Authentication for account authentication
  • Google Cloud Firestore for data storage and synchronization
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications
  • Firebase Analytics for usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash and error reporting
  • Google Sign-In for Google account login

4. Retention

  • Account and synced data are generally retained while the account remains active.
  • Content deleted by the user may remain for a reasonable processing period before full deletion.
  • Local settings and device identifiers may remain on the device until the app is removed or the device storage is reset.
  • Analytics and crash data retention may follow the policies of the relevant service providers.

5. Notifications and Local Storage

The app may request notification permissions for local reminders and push notifications. Some settings are stored locally on the device using local storage mechanisms such as SharedPreferences.

6. International Processing

Tok Tok uses infrastructure services such as Google Firebase, which may process data on servers located outside the Republic of Korea.

7. Your Rights

  • You may review, edit, or delete content you entered in the app.
  • You can change notification permissions in your device settings.
  • You may contact us with privacy-related questions using the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not intentionally collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law. If a parent or guardian believes such information was provided, please contact us.
